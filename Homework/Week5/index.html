WEEK 5

AVERAGE AGE AND LIFE EXPECTANCY

by Civio Arts


Lifetime story

Retirement-policies are changing. New laws arise from the ground.
According to politicians we live longer.
This made me curious. So if we live longer, how long will that be?
How long does the average person still have to live? To get an answer
to this question I went to the 'Centraal Bureau voor Statistiek'. I've got
myself some datasets and tried to visualise it.

The first chart on this website shows the expected lifetime.
When you click on the bar it w ill change the gender.

The second chart will show you the average age in the Netherlands.
When you click on a bar, it will show you the specific gender on the first chart.
For example... if you click on the 'female' bar, the first graph will show you the
expected lifetime of women.

At the top of the webpage you'll see a button. This will change the color of the
chart... just for those who like it more.

Go ahead! Find out how long you have to live!!!
